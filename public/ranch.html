Attractions

Designated Trails

Several cut trails are located a few steps away from your casita and make for easy strolling in a peaceful setting. Of particular interest in the grotto is the Three Brothers Tree with three trees originating from one common trunk. This is named in honor of the owner and his two sibling brothers.

Ranch Livestock

Just south of the casita and the large windmill is our working barn and livestock area. The pasture areas across the cedar post fence are home to our people-friendly, full-time residential livestock.

Fishin Hole

The pond area is a great place to congregate, grill, enjoy a fire in the pit, or picnic. Firewood and lighter fluid needed for both the pit and the grill are provided, as well as utensils needed for the grill.
