Area Offerings

The Wimberley Valley and surrounding areas are known for many diverse activities, in addition to just being a beautiful sightseeing area. The following are some activities you might consider. If you can't find an event you're looking for while you're here, ask Tom! He has lived in Wimberley his whole life and is an expert on the area.

*More specific recommendations are provided when you arrive at your casita.

A Few Recommendations

Dining Options: From food trucks to fine dining, our area offers many choices.

- Ino’z Brew & Chew, Wimberley Square. Open 7 days a week, casual full-service with a full bar, indoor/outdoor seating, weekend live music with a large outdoor deck overlooking beautiful Cypress Creek.

- Hays City Store, FM 3237/FM 150 intersection. Very popular restaurant with a full bar and occasional live music located 10 minutes from the ranch. Turn left when exiting property, then left again at intersection with FM3237.

Wineries, Breweries, and Distilleries: If visiting and sampling the products produced by wineries, distilleries and breweries is on your to do list, you’ve come to the right place. Within 20 miles of Windmill Ranch you will find no less than two dozen of these type businesses. Several wineries are literally within a 10 minute drive of the ranch. One that we highly recommend is Wimberley Valley Winery located a mere quarter mile from us.

Shopping Suggestions: From quaint local shopping to a full-blown Outlet Mall shopping experience, our area offers lots of options. Market Days are a big draw in our area. The first Saturday of each month (except Jan -Feb) over 450 booths open from 7am -3pm. Located on FM 2325.

Located less than a quarter mile towards RR12 on Lone Man Mtn Road is Changes Resale Shop. The shop is part of Life Changes, one of the top estate sale businesses in our area and many of their items come from such sales. Currently their consignment shop is open Wed - Sat.
