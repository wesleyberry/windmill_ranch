Area Offerings

In addition to being a beautiful Hill Country location, the Wimberley Valley and surrounding areas offer many additional attractions. Shopping, wineries, breweries, musical venues, water activities, diverse eateries for every taste, and classic dancehalls are just some of the activities guests may choose from when staying at Windmill Ranch. Guests have the option of staying on site exclusively, a very active off-property experience or any combination in between. It’s up to you!
