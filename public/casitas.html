Casitas

Are you looking for a genuine Wimberley experience? Come stay at Windmill Ranch, where the art and building materials are all local. Your stay is guaranteed to be memorable and comfortable as each casita has delux furnishings and country-like privacy.

Privacy

Our Casitas are located on an 11-acre property in the beautiful Wimberley, TX. Each Casita was built with privacy in mind. Get away from the hustle and bustle of the city and try some country livin'.

Comfort

Our casitas are furnished with the finest amenities, ranging from elite beddings and linens to hot tubs and more.

The Space

The casita at Windmill Ranch offers the best of seclusion while not sacrificing the finer amenities. Large outdoor deck, walking trails, abundant bird watching, secluded hiking trails, dark night skies and brilliant stars, interaction with tame livestock and complete privacy. And this is if you simply stay on the property!
