Casitas

Privacy or Fine Amenities? Don’t sacrifice fine amenities to get the seclusion and privacy you crave. The casita at Windmill Ranch offers the best of both worlds.

Privacy

Your private casita is located within a very private, secluded 11-acre Hill Country ranch. Refresh your spirits as you decompress in a real country setting.

Comfort

At Windmill Ranch, you’ll enjoy the fine amenities designed into your casita. WiFi, Bluetooth indoor/outdoor speakers, quality leather furniture, private hot tub, fire table, full kitchen setup, fine bedroom furnishings featuring top-of-the-line quality king-sized mattress, expensive linens, walk-in dual-headed shower, and many more quality offerings.

The Space

Every detail of our casita was carefully considered keeping in mind comfort and relaxation. We intend to give each person a unique experience that they can remember and cherish. Our goal is to provide you with everything you need so that you can enjoy your stay to the fullest.
